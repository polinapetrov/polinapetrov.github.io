About me

I grew up in the South Hills suburbs of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and feel that I naturally carry some uniquely Pittsburgh traits — a love of pickles, “pop” over “soda”, and time spent as part of a junior Tamburtizans group, to name a few. I’ve always enjoyed exploring different artistic endeavors, which have ranged from dance to drawing & painting to playing the clarinet & the prim to pottery to photography to video editing.

Artsy things

Dance has been my constant through life, my favorite artistic outlet and stress reliever rolled into one. I’ve been dancing for 15+ years and have experience in multiple genres, including ballet, jazz, contemporary, hip hop, tap, musical theater, and heels. While I used to dance competitvely, I just do it for my own enjoyment now. You can find some dances I’ve performed in during my undergrad career here. Creating original choreography is something I’ve explored a lot more in recent years, particularly in contemporary and jazz styles. Some pieces I’ve choreographed live here.

I've always loved traveling, and thanks to my brother who edits commercials and documentaries professionally, sometimes I'm inspired to edit together little music videos encapsulating my trips. You can find some videos I've made here.
